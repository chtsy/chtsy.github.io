I am a PhD Candidate of South China University of Technology and a intern (Jiaqi Wang is my mentor) at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree of Engineering at Yunnan University of China in 2017. My research interest includes Scene Reconstruction, Image/3D Segmentation and Multimodal Large Language Models.
